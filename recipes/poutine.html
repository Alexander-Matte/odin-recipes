Poutine

The Great Canadian Poutine!

Simple, quick and delicious authentic Canadian dish that was founded in rural Quebec in the 1950's. Caution! This can become addicting!!

Ingredients

  • 1 package frozen french fries
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all purpose-flour
  • 1 1/2 cups beef broth
  • Salt and ground pepper to taste
  • 1 1/2 cups cheese curds
